Who We Are

The Peninsula Chinese Business Association is a social and business organization led by a group of Chinese entrepreneurs and leaders in the San Francisco mid-peninsula. We are a 100% volunteer organization with members donating time, talent and resources to strengthen family and community ties in the Peninsula.

Through organized events, we hope to create and develop opportunities for businesses, families and friends to promote and support local schools and communities.

Individuals who are passionate and interested in giving back to the community are welcomed to join our meetings which will provide a forum to network and exchange information.
